  <abstract lang="en">
    <abstract-dtext>
      <p general="">A drop test device includes a base, a limiting member, a lifting assembly, a returning assembly, and a transmission assembly. The limiting member is disposed on the base and configured to receive a test object. The lifting assembly is disposed on the base, the lifting assembly includes a cantilever, a cam and a rotating shaft. The cantilever and the cam are fixedly disposed on the rotating shaft, such that when the rotating shaft rotates, the cantilever and the cam are rotated, and the cantilever lifts and releases the test object during the rotating process. The return assembly is in abutment with the cam, and the cam pushes the return assembly toward the test object during the rotation, thereby pushing the test object to be detected back. The transmission assembly includes a motor coupled to the rotating shaft, and the transmission assembly drives the rotating shaft to rotate.</p>

    <background-art>
      <p general="0002">隨著電子通訊技術的進步，電子裝置(例如手機、平板電腦等)可以滿足大多數情況對娛樂和通信的需求，使得人們需要在多種場合使用電子裝置。因此，為保證產品品質，提升使用者體驗，在電子裝置出廠之前需要對所述電子裝置進行一系列測試，其中包括跌落測試，以驗證電子裝置內模組的可靠程度。</p>
      <p general="0003">傳統的跌落測試裝置通常是採用氣缸驅動夾抓夾持所述電子裝置進行跌落測試。然而，這種跌落測試裝置往往需要多個氣缸配合工作，同時活動結構以及控制電路複雜。</p>
